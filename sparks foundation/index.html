Our Mission

Humanity Gives Orphan Appeal Humanity Gives orphan appeal through our due diligence and partnerships have developed a world wide network of both charitable organizations and orphanages with the view to supporting orphans.
The UNICEF definition of an orphan is a child under 18 years of age who has lost one or both parents to any cause of death There are an estimated 140 million orphans globally with 5.1 million having lost both parents 61 million in Asia, 52 million in Africa, 10 million in Latin America and the Caribbean, and 7.3 million in Eastern Europe and Central Asia. This represents not only children who have lost both parents, but also those that have lost a single parent. 95 per cent of all orphans are over the age of five.
Humanity Gives at present has strong links with orphanages in Morrocco and after meeting our due diligence criteria we are expanding to other countries.
Our intention is to make a difference to the lives of orphans living in poverty. Many of whom have been devastated by abandonment and abuse but are learning to trust and laugh again. We aim to bring compassion to those in need by helping them develop and realize their physical, mental, emotional and true potential.
Your donations will help support the upbringing education and care of an orphan child To date we have supplied medical equipment Toys and clothing and direct financial assistance to our Orphanages However, with your support, we can make a difference To support us, get in touch, by email or donate now clicking the button above.
